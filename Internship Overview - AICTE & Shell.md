## **Internship Overview : AICTE & Shell**

---

This document provides a brief overview of the internship program under which this project was developed. 

### **1\. Program Details** 

* **Program Name :** Shell-Edunet Skills4Future AICTE Internship   
* **Presented By :** Edunet Foundation, in collaboration with AICTE & Shell   
* **Focus Area :** Green Skills using AI technologies   
* **Duration :** 4 Weeks (27th October 2025 \- 27th November 2025\)   
* **Selected Project Theme :** Energy (Prediction) \- Using historical data and machine learning algorithms to forecast future outcomes, such as solar energy forecasting. 

### **2\. Program Purpose** 

* The Shell-Edunet Skills4Future AICTE Internship is designed to help participants develop essential foundational skills needed for productive careers in a rapidly evolving job market.   
* The program provides a unique, project-based learning experience where participants work independently on a project under the guidance of an industry mentor to identify and develop solutions for real-world challenges. 

### **3\. Key Benefits & Objectives** 

* **Mentorship :** Access to personalized mentorship sessions and collaborative group learning.   
* **Project-Based Learning :** An opportunity to expedite learning by building a tangible project prototype.   
* **Industry Exposure :** A holistic learning experience provided by industry experts through knowledge-sharing sessions.   
* **Certification :** A chance to earn certifications from AICTE, Shell, and Edunet, validating technical knowledge and skills.   
* **Showcase :** The opportunity to present project prototypes to a panel of industry experts.
